Morning Devotion

Start your day with purpose, peace, and prayer.
Our Morning Devotion is a daily time of worship, scripture reading, and reflection designed to help you center your heart on God before the busyness of the day begins.



Each session includes:

  • A short worship song or hymn
  • A scripture reading with practical insights
  • Guided prayer and time for personal reflection


Whether you're joining us at the church or tuning in from home, this sacred morning rhythm offers spiritual renewal and a gentle reminder of God's presence throughout your day.

Wednesday Bible Study

Join us every Wednesday evening for a powerful time of studying God's Word together. Our Wednesday Bible Study is open to everyone seeking to grow deeper in their understanding of Scripture, ask questions, and build community in faith.

Each session includes:

  • In-depth teaching on a selected Bible passage or theme
  • Group discussion and Q&A
  • Time of prayer and spiritual encouragement

Whether you're new to the Bible or have studied it for years, this midweek gathering will challenge and refresh your faith. Come with your Bible, your heart open, and a hunger to grow.

Saturday Intercession

Join us every Saturday morning for a time of powerful prayer and spiritual warfare during our Intercession Service. This is a focused gathering where we stand in the gap for our church, families, community, and the nations.

We pray for:

  • Revival in our city and beyond
  • Growth, healing, and unity in the church
  • Personal breakthroughs and spiritual strength
  • Specific needs and requests brought forth by the congregation

Whether you're a seasoned intercessor or just learning to pray with purpose, this is a powerful way to align with God’s heart and see lives changed through prayer.
